Application for teleassistance among health professionals.

Teleassistence APP

Concept & interface planning João Neves
UERJ Telehealth Center develops several projects in the area of telehealth and telemedicine. One of the project was developing of a teleassistance system (teleconsulting + telediagnosis) to assist professionals working in the Unified Health System (SUS).
As part of the team and Design coordinator, I participated in system planning as well as the development of the entire visual concept presented by the application.

Home screen


Service choice

The teleassistance can be divided between Teleconsulting (Second Opinion among professionals) and Telediagnosis (request of health report).

Area Selection Screen (Teleconsulting) IDC-10 / CIAP-2

There are several types of teleconsulting possible to be performed such as Clinical Cases, Work Process etc. As well as various types of professionals available for help.
